Experience Intelligence

The question: does the journey deliver a good outcome the person understood and could act on?

Experience Intelligence is how customers and institutions experience a journey, and whether it delivers good, inclusive outcomes they understand and can act on. An Experience Journey is the application that cuts across the Intelligences; this lens keeps the person, not the transaction, at the centre.

Outcomes it governs

Good outcomes by design

The outcome a journey should produce, defined and tested before launch, not audited after it.

Understanding

Whether people actually understand the choice, the fee and what an agent did for them.

Inclusion and vulnerability

Where a vulnerable customer might struggle, and whether the journey catches them.

Agency and intervention

Whether a person could see what happened and step in when it mattered.

Where we show it

We test propositions for consumer outcomes on a working prototype before a bank commits to build, including the questions only an agentic journey creates. The evidence falls out of the run, ready for a board.
