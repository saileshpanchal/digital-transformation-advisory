Value Intelligence

The question: how is value represented, exchanged, governed, settled and measured?

Value Intelligence covers how value moves and is accounted for, from payments and deposits to tokenised value, contracts and the economics around them. It draws on decades of payments and settlement architecture.

Outcomes it governs

Safe movement

Value moves with the right authority, on the right rail, with evidence at each step.

Settlement and finality

When a payment is final, what can be reversed or disputed, and what the customer can expect.

New forms of value

Tokenised deposits and programmable money, governed with the same care as the balance sheet.

Measured economics

The deposit, the margin and the cost to serve, measured rather than assumed.
