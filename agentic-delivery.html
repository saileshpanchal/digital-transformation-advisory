Agentic Delivery Architecture

The unit of delivery is changing. The future shape is discovered, not forecast.

Traditional delivery split work across specialists. Agile brought them into a two-pizza team. AI changes the unit again: which skills must sit together, and where the boundaries between roles fall. What it does not hand you is a fixed new headcount ratio.

So we sell squads a specific way. Not everyone down to a two-person team, but the smallest safe and effective shape for each workflow, chosen from evidence and supported by an engineered system.

Do not predict the future organisation from today's job titles. Discover it from the work.

Emerging delivery shapes

There is no fixed squad size. Depending on the work, the right shape might be a full multidisciplinary squad, a smaller AI-amplified squad, a domain expert and an engineer, a single accountable professional orchestrating agents, or a deliberately larger team where judgement, customer care or operational risk require it.

We discover the shape, we do not prescribe it

We do not move you to a two-person squad. We find the smallest safe and effective configuration for each workflow, and prove it before we scale it.

We sell squads the DTA way

Most suppliers sell you a team. We sell you the right capability for the problem, and the evidence behind the choice.

Roles before bodies

We define the roles the outcome needs against SFIA, then assemble the smallest shape that can own the work. You buy the right capability for the problem, not seats.

The choice is evidenced

We pick the shape from how the work actually runs, and record why. Defensible when the board or the regulator asks, and revisable when the evidence changes.

Run inside our discipline

Every engagement runs inside DTA's operating model, with an accountable DTA lead, under the same decision and evidence discipline as our Accountable Bank demonstration.

One shared system. Many possible shapes.

The platform does not assume a team size. It supplies context, constraints, evidence, reusable knowledge and escalation to whatever shape the work needs, from a full team to a single accountable professional.
