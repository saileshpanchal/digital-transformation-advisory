Why we exist

Agents are crossing from advice to action.
The real question is whether you can answer for it.

Adopting AI tools is no longer the hard part. The hard part is acting through them safely: a regulated bank can only let an agent move money or run a journey if it is accountable for every action that agent takes, and sovereign over the agents, data and models it runs on. You cannot be accountable for what you do not control.

That is accountable banking, and it is what we build with you: deploy agentic product squads, ship real AI propositions, stand up the platforms they run on — and leave the capability with you. Not advice. Not a vendor's black-box OS you rent.

How we work

For wider change, start senior and scale deliberately.

A standalone squad can start immediately. Proposition and transformation work follows a deliberate path from discovery to proof and scale.

Discover

Founder-led discovery, drawing on senior enterprise, business and domain architecture. We identify the value drivers, proposition, domains and organisational changes that justify using AI. This is senior advisory work, not squad delivery.

Pilot: the Glass Tube

The pilot is a Glass Tube, sized to the question. A light version lands a Product Owner and Forward Platform Engineer, plus selected enterprise-service specialists where needed, to build a working prototype in a controlled sandbox. A fuller version runs the first domain end to end, adding the squad, coaches, controls and business functions needed to prove the operating model.

Scale

Scale what the Glass Tube has proved. Multiple squads take ownership of the Domain Building Blocks, while coaches and specialists across finance, HR, procurement, risk and audit extend the model through the organisation.

The operating model

Grow the delivery model with the work

A squad can be consumed on its own or become part of a wider change. Discovery is led by DTA's senior leadership and architects. The Glass Tube pilot can be light or full-depth, drawing on selected enterprise services. At scale, durable squads own the Domain Building Blocks.

Leadership

Sailesh Panchal — Founder & CEO

Sailesh is a technology, payments and transformation leader in UK financial services and FinTech. As CTO and Payments & Economic Crime Director at TSB Bank, he led a bank-wide transformation that brought technology and business leadership closer together. It simplified the cost base, accelerated delivery, reduced fraud, supported growth in payments, unified architecture and established the bank's AI Centre of Excellence.

He has built regulated banks from the ground up and shaped national infrastructure. As Group CTO at Orwell Group he conceived the ipagoo platform — the first cloud-first regulated EMI certified by the FCA and ACPR on AWS and Azure, live on the UK payment schemes in months and winner of the 2018 FIA Award. As Chief Architect for the Payment Systems Regulator he led the New Payments Architecture, and he has contributed to Bank of England, ISO 20022 and Open Banking committees.

Earlier roles include Director of Engineering at Metro Bank (Agile/SAFe at scale), Head of Regulatory Architecture at Lloyds Banking Group (a £250m+ portfolio spanning FSCS, CASS, payM and PSD2/Open Banking), CTO at Travelex and Chief Architect at JPMorgan. He founded Digital Transformation Advisory in 2019 and is writing a book on digital transformation in UK banking.
