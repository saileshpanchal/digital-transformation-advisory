Why we exist

Agents are crossing from advice to action.
The real question is whether you can answer for it.

Adopting AI tools is no longer the hard part. The hard part is acting through them safely: a regulated bank can only let an agent move money or run a journey if it is accountable for every action that agent takes, and sovereign over the agents, data and models it runs on. You cannot be accountable for what you do not control.

That is accountable banking, and it is what we build with you: deploy agentic product squads, ship real AI propositions, stand up the platforms they run on — and leave the capability with you. Not advice. Not a vendor's black-box OS you rent.

Leadership

Sailesh Panchal — Founder & CEO

A technology, payments and transformation leader with 25+ years in UK financial services and FinTech, and £1bn+ of payments and transformation programmes delivered. He is currently CTO and Payments & Economic Crime Director at TSB Bank — a Material Risk Taker accountable for five Important Business Services — where he leads Evolve, the bank-wide transformation that broke down the silos between technology and the business: £20m+ annual cost savings, IT change cost down ~25%, delivery moved from quarterly to monthly, a £6.8m fraud reduction and +30% international-payments revenue, alongside a unified architecture function reporting to the Board and an AI Centre of Excellence.

He has built regulated banks from the ground up and shaped national infrastructure. As Group CTO at Orwell Group he conceived the ipagoo platform — the first cloud-first regulated EMI certified by the FCA and ACPR on AWS and Azure, live on the UK payment schemes in months and winner of the 2018 FIA Award. As Chief Architect for the Payment Systems Regulator he led the New Payments Architecture, and he has contributed to Bank of England, ISO 20022 and Open Banking committees.

Earlier roles include Director of Engineering at Metro Bank (Agile/SAFe at scale), Head of Regulatory Architecture at Lloyds Banking Group (a £250m+ portfolio spanning FSCS, CASS, payM and PSD2/Open Banking), CTO at Travelex and Chief Architect at JPMorgan. He founded Digital Transformation Advisory in 2019 and is writing a book on digital transformation in UK banking.
