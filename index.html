Experience Journeys

An Experience Journey is the end-to-end human application that cuts across the Intelligences. We design them, prove them on the demonstration with test consumers, and evidence the outcomes before a bank commits to build. Each one shows an agent preparing and proposing, a human authorising material action, and the evidence recorded at every step.

Architecture, proven before it is built

We define an independent target architecture and prove the hard parts first, so the platform conforms to the architecture rather than the other way round. Our reference architecture for governed human-machine action is Capability, Context and Consent (C3), with Domain Building Blocks beneath it. Because the work is expressed in open standards, ISO 20022, BIAN and recognised control frameworks, what we prove becomes a portable, vendor-neutral working specification you can build with any vendor.

Accountable Bank: our demonstration

Accountable Bank is our working proving ground: one governed, agent-native platform that runs several banking propositions, built to production-grade controls. We use it to test whether a proposition is likely to deliver good outcomes before a bank commits to build, and to produce the working specification that comes out the other side.

A demonstration on synthetic data, with test consumers, not a regulated bank. See how it works.

Leadership

Sailesh Panchal, Founder & CEO

Sailesh is a technology, payments and transformation leader in UK financial services and FinTech. As CTO and Payments & Economic Crime Director at TSB Bank, he led a bank-wide transformation that brought technology and business leadership closer together. It simplified the cost base, accelerated delivery, reduced fraud, supported growth in payments, unified architecture and established the bank's AI Centre of Excellence.

He has built regulated banks from the ground up and shaped national infrastructure. As Group CTO at Orwell Group he conceived the ipagoo platform, the first cloud-first regulated EMI certified by the FCA and ACPR on AWS and Azure, live on the UK payment schemes in months and winner of the 2018 FIA Award. As Chief Architect for the Payment Systems Regulator he led the New Payments Architecture, and he has contributed to Bank of England, ISO 20022 and Open Banking committees.

Earlier roles include Director of Engineering at Metro Bank (Agile/SAFe at scale), Head of Regulatory Architecture at Lloyds Banking Group (a £250m+ portfolio spanning FSCS, CASS, payM and PSD2/Open Banking), CTO at Travelex and Chief Architect at JPMorgan. He founded Digital Transformation Advisory in 2019 and is writing a book on digital transformation in UK banking.
