Architecture

We define an independent target architecture and prove the hard parts first, so the platform conforms to the architecture rather than the other way round. The work is expressed in open standards (ISO 20022, BIAN, recognised control frameworks), so what we prove becomes a portable, vendor-neutral working specification you can build with any vendor.
