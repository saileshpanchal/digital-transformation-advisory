Platform Intelligence

How systems evolve and compose: interoperability, coexistence, governed data, and the runtime architecture that lets old and new operate together.

Will this still hold after the next platform change?

Replacing the core is not the same as transforming the bank. This lens reads how systems compose and evolve, how old and new run together during migration, and how data stays governed across the change, so a decision survives the next generation of technology rather than locking the bank into it.

What this lens governs

Evolution and composability

How capabilities are composed and replaced without rebuilding the bank each time.

Interoperability

Open standards as the shared language, so components plug in and can be swapped.

Coexistence

Running the old and new worlds together safely while value migrates.

Governed data

Data products that carry meaning, authority, and lineage rather than scattered tables.
