Trust Intelligence

How justified trust is earned among consumers, institutions, and regulators, through inclusion, consent, privacy, evidence, and resilience.

{% include cta-calendly.html kind="strategy_call" loc="trust-int-hero" label="Discuss a challenge" %} See all Intelligences

Has this earned the trust it asks for?

Trust is an outcome, not a setting. This lens treats trust as something a bank earns from consumers, institutions, and regulators by being inclusive, asking consent, protecting privacy, producing evidence, and staying resilient. The control mechanisms belong to Control Intelligence; trust is what those mechanisms have to earn.

What this lens governs

Inclusion

Whether the people most easily excluded can take part safely, and on fair terms.

Consent and privacy

Authority and consent carried as first-class facts, and sensitive information kept to its purpose.

Evidence

Evidence produced as decisions are made, so trust rests on record rather than reassurance.

Resilience

The confidence that the system keeps its promises when something fails.
