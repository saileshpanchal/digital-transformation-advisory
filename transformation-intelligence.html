The category, explained

Transformation Intelligence

Traditional consulting helps you decide what to build. Transformation Intelligence helps you decide whether it should be built at all, what evidence is required before committing, and how to demonstrate that evidence before delivery begins.

Lenses inside. Doors outside.

Inside, the Eight Intelligences are lenses: every transformation question is read through all eight at once. Ignoring one does not remove it; it moves the problem into production. Outside, each is a door: a named problem for a named executive, with a 90-Day Assessment. You enter through one door, never the whole building.
