The Transformation Intelligence Workbench

Every page here is a view of one model

Transformation Intelligence is the discipline of making, governing and continuously improving organisational decisions. Organisational cognition emerges from the quality, connectedness and evidence of those decisions. Everything else, process, capability, knowledge, architecture, AI and governance, is a projection of the same Decision Graph.

Open the Family Wealth model Run the live demonstrations

Three things, kept separate

The discipline

Transformation Intelligence. Decisions are the first-class object; cognition emerges from their quality, connectedness and evidence.

The methodology

Decision-first transformation. Observe, model the decisions, connect them, prove the change, transform, then keep learning.

The platform

The Workbench. It stores the decisions, knowledge, evidence, architecture, AI and governance as one connected model.

This website demonstrates the methodology. The software implements it.

Everything derives from decisions

Start with the decision an executive is trying to make. Everything else exists to enable, constrain or evidence it.
