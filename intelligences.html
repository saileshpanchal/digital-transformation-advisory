The Eight Intelligences

Transformation Intelligence is what DTA does: turning major change into a series of proven decisions. The Eight Intelligences are how we deliver it — complementary lenses that produce the evidence before an organisation commits capital, technology or customers.

They are how we see the work: overlapping lenses, not filing folders. A single journey, proposition or piece of architecture can sit under several at once. They do not replace our doctrine (Owned Autonomy), our reference architecture (C3), or our capability units (Domain Building Blocks); they connect them.

This is the master view. Each lens has its own page for depth, and the journeys show several working together, so the model stays coherent as the thinking evolves.

Why eight, and why they overlap

Real journeys do not sit in one box. Each draws on several Intelligences at once, which is why we read every piece of work through all eight rather than filing it under one.
