Experience Journeys

A journey is the end-to-end experience a person or institution actually lives. We use journeys to test propositions, authority boundaries, data products, and consumer outcomes before a bank commits to build.

A journey is not the same as a proposition

A journey is the experience a person or institution lives, end to end. A proposition is the commercial or product concept a bank sells. One proposition can contain several journeys, and a single journey usually draws on several Intelligences at once. We lead with the journey because that is where outcomes are won or lost, and because it exposes the assumptions a product description hides.

The journeys we work with

Each carries an honest evidence label. A demonstration genuinely runs on the Clean-Room platform with synthetic data. A blueprint is designed and specified, not yet demonstrated end to end.
