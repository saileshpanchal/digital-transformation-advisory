{%- comment -%} The squad evolution: four stages, traditional stack to the 1:1 agentic cell. Native. {%- endcomment -%}
1

Traditional stack

Work passes through specialist layers, business to operations.

  • Many hand-offs
  • Slow feedback
2

Two-pizza squad

A persistent cross-functional team.

  • Broader ownership
  • Coordination burden remains
3

Hybrid agentic squad

A smaller human team uses agents inside existing delivery.

  • AI-assisted
  • Existing process remains
4

1:1 agentic cell

A domain authority and a Forward Deployed Engineer, amplified by agents.

  • Outcome-owned
  • Evidence-led
What changes
Team size shrinks Capability becomes reusable Controls move into the platform The workflow becomes the unit of transformation
